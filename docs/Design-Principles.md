Tradecraft Labs Design Principles

1. Research is the product.

2. The platform should feel academic, professional, and modern.

3. Research artifacts are first-class content.

4. Show the evolution of research, not just final outputs.

5. Prioritize clarity over visual complexity.

6. Avoid cybersecurity clichés and hacker aesthetics.

7. Design for researchers, practitioners, executives, and students simultaneously.

8. Every page should answer:
   - What is this?
   - Why does it matter?
   - What should I do next?

9. Build a research platform, not a website.

10. Future research projects should require little or no architectural change.
