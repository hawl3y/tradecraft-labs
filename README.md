# tradecraft-labs

Independent research platform documenting cybersecurity governance, leadership, and decision-quality research.

## Overview

Tradecraft Labs is an independent research initiative focused on exploring the human side of cybersecurity governance, leadership, and risk-informed decision making.

The platform is designed to document and publish cybersecurity research from initial concept through publication, providing visibility into the full research lifecycle rather than only completed papers.

Tradecraft Labs serves as both a research repository and a public research journal, capturing the evolution of ideas, theories, findings, and practical insights as they develop.

## Current Research

### Project 001

**The Business Information Security Officer as a Governance Intermediary: Improving Cybersecurity Decision Quality Through Trust, Influence, and Risk-Based Decision Making**

This research explores how Business Information Security Officers (BISOs) create value within modern enterprises and examines the relationship between cybersecurity governance, trust, influence, and risk-informed decision making.

## Philosophy

Good cybersecurity outcomes depend on more than technology alone.

Tradecraft Labs explores how governance, leadership, trust, and organizational decision making influence cybersecurity outcomes and how practitioners navigate competing priorities through structured, risk-informed approaches.

Build a research platform, not a website.
