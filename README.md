# Tradecraft Labs

Independent research exploring the human side of cybersecurity governance, leadership, and risk-informed decision making.

## About

Tradecraft Labs publishes research in progress: working theories, raw observations, literature frameworks, and evolving analysis as it develops. Research is the product, not only the finished paper.

## Current Research

**Project 001 — The BISO as a Governance Intermediary**

Examines how the Business Information Security Officer (BISO) functions as a governance intermediary and boundary-spanning leadership role that enables organizations to navigate competing cybersecurity and business priorities through structured risk-based decision making.

Stage: Practitioner Interviews

## Platform

Built with Next.js 15, Contentlayer2, and Tailwind CSS. Statically generated and deployed on Vercel.
